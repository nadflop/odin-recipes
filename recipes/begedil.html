Begedil (Fried Potato Patties)

Desciption

Begedil are delicious fried potato patties with a meltingly soft middle that is a popular side dish in Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia and Brunei.

Ingredients

  • 1/2kg of potatoes, peeled and washed
  • 200g of miched meatc/chicken (can omit this if you're vegetarian)
  • Half bunch of cilantro, chopped finely
  • 1/2 cup of fried shallots
  • 2 eggs (beaten, used for coating when deep frying later)
  • White pepper
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Oil (enough for frying)

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes until its fork tender, drain the water and put aside in a dry bowl and mash them
  2. While waiting for the potatoes, you can start cooking the minced meat by frying them without any oil (can skip this step if you're vegetarian)
  3. Throw in the chopped cilantro, fried shallots and meat into the mashed potatoes. Mix them well.
  4. Season the mix with white pepper, black pepper and salt. Shape it into medium sized balls with the palm of your hands before flattening it.
  5. Coat the potato balls in the beaten eggs and fry until lightly browned.
